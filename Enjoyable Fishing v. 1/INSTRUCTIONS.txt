Hello! Thanks for downloading the Loot Table!

Follow these steps, to add the loot table to your world:

1. Start Minecraft 1.9 or later
2. Select the world, you want to add the world to
3. Click on "Edit"
4. Click on "Open Folder".
5. Open the "data"-folder in the folder, that just opened
6. Put the "loot_tables" in the data folder

That's it! Now Fishing should be advanced in your world!
